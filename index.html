Priorities for Ambrosia

Cut the Vehicle Tax

Reduce the established large vehicle tax, Ambrosians should not be penalized for simply going to work, school or market.

Expand Transport Connections

Investing in roads, rail and infrastructure to better connect Ambrosia's communities, and to the rest of Caprica.

Support for Farmers

Provide targeted, responsible support for families to uphold stability, improve productivity and strengthen Ambrosia's agricultural economy.

Community Safety

Strengthen and support community law enforcement for maintaining safe, stable communities across Ambrosia.

Local Control

Ensuring decisions affecting Ambrosia are made closer to home, with greater input from local communities.
